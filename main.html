Destination:Patna


Patnais a city in the state of "Bihar" in the eastern part of India , and is the capital of the state of Bihar , that previosly had name Patliputra, located on the banks of river Ganges.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. The holy river Ganga: The city has evolved and developed near the banks of the river Ganga.
  2. The Golghar: The golghar is historic architecture built with no pillar ,in 1776 under then governal general Sir Warren Hastings to store Grain at tme of famine, Comleted in 1786 built by Engineer John Garstin, with around strucure and 141 number of steps to reach to the Top of the dome or beehive like structure.
  3. The Patna Museum: Patna city has two museum , the old and the new , both the mueseum has good amount of the historical collection and the new mueseum is considered as one of the best mueseum in India, has collection as old as 3 B.C.E
  4. The Patna Zoo: Patna zoo is cosidered as the lungs of the city , and has collection of diversed species of animals, reptiles and plants , together patna zoo is a biodiversity of natural existence,The name of Patna zoo is Sanjay Gandhi Jaivik Udhyan .
  5. Danapur Cantonment: Danapur cantonment is considered as one of the oldest cantonment of India about 250 + old record of establisment .

Must try food:

  • Poori, sabji , Jalebi :One of the most commonly severed breakfast in food outlets , is combination of sweet and salty both , sufficient for one time appetite.
  • kachori sabji and jalebi: Again the food is served in morning as a breakfast , good and must try .
